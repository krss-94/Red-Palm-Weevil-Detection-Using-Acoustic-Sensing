DETECTION PIPELINE

🔩
1
PIEZO INPUT
27mm PZT-5A disc bonded to M6 screw. LM358 amplifier — 20 dB gain.
ADC · 12-bit · GPIO34
〰️
2
IIR HPF
First-order high-pass filter at 200 Hz. Kills DC and low-freq vibration noise.
α = 0.9355
⚡
3
TKEO
Teager-Kaiser Energy Operator. ψ[n] = x[n]² − x[n+1]·x[n−1]
Instantaneous energy
📊
4
ADAPTIVE THRESH
T = max(μ + 3σ, P95). Welford's online algorithm — no 240k sample buffer.
Welford · P95 subset
🦟
5
BURST COUNT
200ms grouping, 500ms inter-burst gap, ≥3 crossings/burst. Rejects mechanical impacts.
Pinhas et al. 2008

State Machine

DETECTION STATES

NORMAL
0 bursts detected
No chewing activity detected in the 30-second window. Green LED. Node sleeps for 4.5 minutes.
SUSPICIOUS
1–2 bursts · or 3× consecutive
Low-level activity detected. Amber LED. After 3 consecutive SUSPICIOUS windows, escalates automatically to INFESTED.
INFESTED
≥3 bursts · persistence escalation
High-confidence infestation. Red LED + triple buzzer beep. LoRa alert fired to gateway. Call agricultural officer.
